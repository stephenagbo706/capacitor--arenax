ArenaX Community Chat – Features

  • General Chat Room: Global room plus optional FIFA/eFootball/DLS/COD subrooms supporting text, emojis, and formatting.
  • Player Profiles & Mentions: Tap a username to view profile, mention @players, follow them, or start private chats.
  • Real-Time Messaging: Messages, typing indicators, and online/offline status update instantly (via WebSockets/Firebase).
  • Admin & Moderation Tools: Admins can pin/delete/ban and auto-moderation filters block spam/offensive links.
  • Chat Features: Copy, reply, edit/delete your own messages, and optionally share images/screenshots.
  • Notifications: Alerts for new community chat messages plus optional game or tournament updates.
